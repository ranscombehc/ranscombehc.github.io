Ranscombe Holiday Cottage
Rated "Exceptional"
9.9 out of 10 on Booking.com

Ranscombe Holiday Cottage is a luxurious, self contained, self-catering property overlooking the beautiful communal gardens, pond area and orchards of The Manor, with stunning countryside beyond.

With its large open plan living room and conservatory downstairs, it is triple aspect, full of light, and has magnificent vistas on every side. The huge level lawn beside the cottage makes a fantastic play area and there is a sunny terrace for alfresco dining beside the conservatory.

Children are very welcome. Pets are accepted.

The kitchen is fully equipped with a large range cooker, fridge, freezer, dishwasher and washer tumble dryer.

Upstairs there are two bedrooms, a double and one that can be twin or double, bright and airy with quirky round windows and sloping ceilings. The bathroom has an overhead shower and serves both bedrooms.
